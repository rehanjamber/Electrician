Detailed Technical Services

Installation & Upgrades

  • Wiring & Rewiring: Whole-home wiring, room additions, and replacing outdated, unsafe aluminum wiring.
  • Electrical Panels: Main breaker panel upgrades (e.g., 200-amp service), sub-panels, and fuse box replacement.
  • Lighting Systems: Indoor (recessed, chandeliers), outdoor landscape, and security/motion-sensor lighting.
  • Outlets & Switches: GFCI/AFCI safety outlets, dimmers, 240V heavy appliance outlets, and USB/smart outlets.
  • Smart Home Tech: Smart thermostats, lighting relays, doorbell cameras, and automation hubs.
  • Surge Protection: Whole-house surge protectors wired directly at the main panel.

Repair & Maintenance

  • Troubleshooting: Diagnosing flickering lights, dead outlets, buzzing switches, and tripping breakers.
  • Safety Inspections: Code compliance, home buyer evaluations, and safety audits.
  • Short Circuits: Immediate fault finding and repair for short circuits to prevent fire hazards.

Specialty Appliance Hookups

Providing safe, high-voltage power sources even when other trades are involved.

  • Electric Water Heaters: Heavy-duty 240V circuits and disconnects for traditional and tankless units.
  • Water Pumps: Wiring for sump pumps, ejector pumps, and integration with battery backups.
  • Kitchen Appliances: Dedicated circuits for heavy ovens, microwaves, garbage disposals, and dishwashers.
  • Outdoor Leisure: Code-compliant circuits for hot tubs, spas, and pools (GFCI protection, equipment bonding).

24/7 Emergency Services

Immediate response for severe fire and shock hazards:

  • Burning smells from outlets, switches, or panels.
  • Sparking, smoking, or visibly damaged components.
  • Sudden total power loss (non-WAPDA outage).
  • Repeatedly tripping main breakers.
  • Storm damage to exterior weatherheads or meters.
